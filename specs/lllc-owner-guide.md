## First Things First - Save This Link

Your club's page is at:

**manitoubeachmichigan.com/ladies-club**

**Right now, before you do anything else:**
On your phone, open that page, then add it to your Home Screen so you always have it. Here's how:

*On iPhone:*
1. Go to the page in Safari
2. Tap the little box with an arrow at the bottom of your screen (it looks like a box with an upward arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add** in the top right corner
5. Now your club's page is on your phone like an app!

*On Android:*
1. Go to the page in Chrome
2. Tap the three dots in the top right corner
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
4. Tap **Add**

## How to Share Your Page on Facebook

When you want to let people know about Summerfest, here's the easiest way:

**Step 1:** Open your club's page: `manitoubeachmichigan.com/ladies-club`

**Step 2:** Copy the link.
- On your phone: tap the address bar at the top where you see the web address. Tap and hold it, then tap **Copy** when it appears.

**Step 3:** Open Facebook.

**Step 4:** Start a new post. Tap "What's on your mind?" at the top of your feed.

**Step 5:** Tap and hold in the typing area, then tap **Paste**. The link will appear.

**Step 6:** Facebook will automatically show a preview of your page - it should show your club's logo and a nice preview. You'll see it appear below your typing area.

**Step 7:** Type something friendly above the link. For example:
> *"Come join us for Summerfest 2026! All the details are right here - music, food, arts, crafts, and so much more. See you June 20th! 🌻"*

**Step 8:** Tap **Post**.

That's it! You just shared your page with the whole community.

## How to Send Your Page in a Text Message

This one is even easier.

**Step 1:** Copy your club's link: `manitoubeachmichigan.com/ladies-club`

**Step 2:** Open a text message to whoever you want to send it to.

**Step 3:** Tap and hold in the message box, tap **Paste**.

**Step 4:** Hit send!

The link will show up as a clickable link for them. When they tap it, your beautiful page opens right up.

## How to Get a New Sponsor Added to Your Page

Great news - there's a form right on your page for this! You don't have to call Daryl every time someone wants to sponsor.

**Here's what you tell sponsors:**

*"Go to manitoubeachmichigan.com/ladies-club and scroll down to the section that says 'Become a Sponsor.' Fill out the form right there. Someone from the club will follow up with you within 2 business days."*

Or you can send them the direct link to jump right to the form:

**manitoubeachmichigan.com/ladies-club#ladies-sponsor-form**

*(That #ladies-sponsor-form at the end tells the page to scroll straight to the form for them.)*

**What happens after they fill it out:**
1. You'll get an email notification that someone submitted the form
2. Review it, confirm the sponsorship is real
3. Contact Daryl to add them to the sponsor section on the page
