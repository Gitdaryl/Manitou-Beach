Truck name, what you serve, email, phone. That's it for required fields. You can also drop in your website or Instagram (it links straight from your live card so customers find your menu) and upload a truck photo or logo. The photo's optional but it makes your card look great.

Every Time You Head to the Lake
Open your link. Tap one button. You're on the map.
1
Open your check-in link
The one we texted you. It opens right in your phone's browser — no app, no login screen, nothing.
2
Drop a location note
"Near the boat launch." "By the park pavilion." "Corner of the lot by the big tree." Whatever helps people find you. Quick and casual.
3
Add today's special (optional but smart)
Running a lunch deal? Testing a new item? Add it here — it shows as a highlighted badge on your card. Customers browsing the locator see it immediately. That's the thing that turns "maybe later" into "let's go now."

4
Tap "I'm Here" — you're live
Your map pin goes live. Live Now badge, your location, your special. Anyone checking the locator — or getting a notification — sees you in seconds. When you leave, you come down. Simple.
Pro move: pre-announce before you leave home
Planning to be at Manitou Saturday? Open your link and set a Coming Date. Customers browsing the locator see "Coming This Saturday" on your card before you even hitch up. You show up to a crowd instead of building one. That's the difference between hoping for foot traffic and creating demand.
