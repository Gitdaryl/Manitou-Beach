# Manitou Beach Michigan — Social Media Drafts
**Prepared:** May 2026 | **Channels:** Facebook (Manitou Beach Michigan) + Instagram (@manitoubeachmi)

---

## CONTENT STRATEGY OVERVIEW

**Phase 1 goal:** Awareness. People don't know the site exists. Every post for the next 30 days is a soft introduction — not a sales pitch, just "hey, this place is awesome and there's a site for it."

**Audience buckets:**
1. Locals and seasonal residents — they're proud of this place, they'll share
2. Visitors from Toledo, Detroit, Columbus, Chicago — the Tip-Up Town Toledo effect, replicated all summer
3. Business owners, event organizers, food trucks, renters — they want exposure, give it to them free first

**Post cadence:** 3x per week to start
- Thursday 9am — "This Weekend" roundup (events, food trucks, what's on)
- Tuesday mid-morning — Feature/discovery (a business, a trail, a thing to do)
- Saturday late morning — Vibe/lifestyle (pure eye candy, lake life, the feeling of this place)
