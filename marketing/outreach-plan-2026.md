## The Anti-Overlap Rule

**One business = one agent. Forever.**

Before contacting any business, the agent claims it in the tracker app. If a business is already claimed, it's off-limits regardless of how well you know them. No exceptions. Two people calling the same business makes the whole operation look amateur.

**Dispute resolution:** If two agents both know a business well, Outreach Lead gets priority. She's the paid professional.

## Territory Assignment

### Outreach Lead — Business Acquisition

**Her territory:** Any business she personally knows from the old MB website or from living in the area. Also: ALL cold outreach to businesses neither of the other two agents has a personal relationship with.

**Why she owns the cold list:** She's known to local business owners as someone who's been invested in Manitou Beach for years. "I used to run the old Manitou Beach website" positions her as a community builder returning to her roots - not a salesperson. Business owners remember her.

**Immediate first task:** Work through the businesses seeded in the tracker app. Start with any she personally knows or has history with from the old website - those are the warmest leads on the list.

**Her advantage over everyone:** She's a recognizable face in the community. Business owners who were on the old site already trust her. She's not introducing a stranger - she's a familiar voice calling about something she built before.

**Target:** 10-15 newly claimed/contacted businesses per week.

**Activity types (in order of effectiveness):**
1. In-person drop-in (best - 5-10 min walk-in during slow business hours)
2. Phone call (second best - personal, direct)
3. Email (follow-up after call, or for businesses with no good call-time)
4. Facebook Business Page message (last resort for businesses that are hard to reach)

---

### Community Connector — Network & Social

**Her territory:** Daryl's and her personal network. Facebook community groups. Cottage/vacation rental owners (harder to reach in person). Businesses connected to the community through personal relationships rather than commerce.

**Primary channels:**
- Facebook groups: Manitou Beach MI, Devils Lake Michigan, Irish Hills area groups
- Instagram DMs to local businesses that are active on IG
- Personal messages to people she knows who own or manage businesses
- Word of mouth referrals from listed businesses ("do you know anyone else who should be on here?")

**Her advantage:** She doesn't feel like a sales call. She's a community member sharing something she believes in. That's the most trusted form of outreach.

**Key message for Facebook groups:**
> "Hey everyone - my husband built a new community platform for Manitou Beach. It's free to list your business and it's already getting [X] visitors a month. If you own a business around the lake - food truck, rental, service, restaurant, anything - you should be on it. Check it out at manitoubeachmichigan.com"

Post this in: Manitou Beach community groups, Devils Lake area groups, Irish Hills buy/sell/trade groups, local event groups.

**Target:** 5-8 new leads per week (quality over quantity - these are warm referrals).

**Special task - vacation rentals:** There are dozens of private cabin and cottage rentals around the lake that aren't on Airbnb or VRBO. Owners often rent through Facebook Marketplace or word of mouth. She should DM every one she finds. The /stays page is built and these listings cost the owner nothing.

---

### Follow-Up Agent — The Second Touch

**Her territory:** The follow-up queue. Any business that Chelsea or Erin has contacted but hasn't gotten a response from in 5+ business days.

**Why she's the follow-up person:** A second call from a different, warmer voice lands differently than the same person calling again. It also signals that the community platform has more than one person behind it - it's a real operation.

**Her script:**
> "Hi, my name is Amy - I'm calling about the Manitou Beach community platform. I think Chelsea may have reached out to you recently? I just wanted to follow up and see if you had any questions. It's completely free to get listed and it takes about 5 minutes."

**Also her territory:** Churches, faith communities, and seasonal organizations. These are relationship-driven, slower-moving, and suit her warm approach. They're also often the most loyal once they're in.

**Target:** Follow up on 100% of contacted-but-no-response businesses within 5 business days.

## The Status Ladder

Every business goes through these stages. Track in the app.

| Status | What it means | Who acts next |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| **New** | On the list, not yet contacted | Assigned agent |
| **Contacted** | First touch made (call/email/message) | Follow-Up Agent if no response in 5 days |
| **Interested** | Business expressed interest, hasn't listed yet | Assigned agent sends listing link + helps set up |
| **Listed - Free** | They have a free profile | Daryl (30-day stats email) |
| **Listed - Paid** | They upgraded to $9/$25/$49 | Daryl (ongoing) |
| **No Interest** | Politely declined | Archive - check back in 3 months |
| **Not Available** | Couldn't reach, no response after 3 attempts | Follow-Up Agent tries a different channel |

## The Scripts

### Opening (phone call)

**Chelsea's version:**
> "Hi, this is Chelsea - I used to run the old Manitou Beach website. I'm calling because we've rebuilt it as a proper community platform and I wanted to make sure [business name] was on it. It's completely free to have a listing and it's already getting good traffic. Do you have two minutes?"

**Erin's version (personal network):**
> "Hey, it's Erin - Daryl's wife. He built this new website for Manitou Beach and I'm helping get local businesses listed. It's completely free to start and you'd be one of the first on there. Can I send you the link?"

**Follow-Up Agent version:**
> "Hi, I think someone from our team may have been in touch recently about the Manitou Beach website? I'm just following up to see if you had any questions. Takes about 5 minutes to get listed and it's free."

### Handling "What is it?"

> "It's the new community platform for Manitou Beach - like a local directory, but it also has the events calendar, the food truck map, the wine trail, places to stay... everything in one place. Visitors use it to find things to do, locals use it to know what's happening. Your listing is free - we just put your name, phone number, and what you do. If you want to add photos and hours it's $9 a month but that's completely optional."

### Handling "We already have a website / Facebook page"

> "That's great - a lot of people just add this as a second presence. The platform gets traffic from people searching for things to do at the lake who've never heard of your business. It's basically a second door for new customers. And since it's free, there's nothing to lose by being on it."

### Handling "How much does it cost?"

> "Free to start - we just list your name and contact info. If you want photos, hours, and a fuller profile it's $9 a month. The top tier is $49 and includes setting up your Google Business Profile, but you don't need to think about any of that right now. Free is the starting point."

### Handling "I'll think about it"

> "No problem at all. Can I send you the link so you can see what it looks like? [Get their email or text number.] I'll send you a couple of profiles that are already live so you can see exactly what your listing would look like."

## The Listing Handoff

When a business says yes, here's what happens:

1. Agent sends them: `manitoubeachmichigan.com/featured` and tells them to click "List Your Business Free"
2. If they struggle with technology: agent offers to fill it in for them (takes 5 min on a phone call)
3. Agent logs "Interested → Listed" in the tracker app
4. Daryl gets a notification (via tracker) and manually verifies/activates the listing in Notion
5. Daryl sends a personal thank-you message: "You're live - here's your listing: [link]"

1. **Old website users** - She knows who was on the old MB website. These people already believe in the concept. They're the hottest leads on the list. Call them first - this week, before anything else.
